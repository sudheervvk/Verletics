+

+
Not at all! Whether you’re a weekend runner, a competitive amateur, or aiming for professional-level performance, the system adjusts to your level. Beginners get structured guidance, while advanced athletes benefit from progressive periodization and smarter load management.

Our AI analyzes your training logs, performance data, and goals to create tailored drills and progression. Over time, it detects patterns in how you recover, adapt, and perform—so your training becomes increasingly personal and effective.
